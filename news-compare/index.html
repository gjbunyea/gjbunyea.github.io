News-Compare

In this quiz, you'll read some quotes from articles
about education from various news sources.
Try and guess who said the quote and see how
good you are at determining bias out of context.
The quotes chosen are meant to be intentionally vague

This is meant to be a test of exposure. Do you read
multiple news sources? Do you know your own biases?
Are you a victim of echo chambering?

Good Luck and Happy Reading!
